About Us

This is Our Group.

We are just college students. who are making a application to provide some food and financial support to needy people and NGOs. From this application Benefactor we want to create a social change in our society. Where others can help poor children to study. And from these we can see a better INDIA.

View more

All our initiatives are designed to provide essential food support to underserved communities in the form of raw grains, freshly cooked food or packaged food products depending on the need. Our aim is to ensure, hunger never comes in the way of a brighter future.
